Why AI Slide Iteration Breaks
  • Layout drift from partial updates
  • Inconsistent design rules across iterations
1) Layout drift from partial updates
Edits move objects incrementally, not as a wholeText reflow changes bounds and spacing
Constraints are violated (alignment, margins)Small differences compound across iterations
Result: each “improvement” subtly breaks the previous version.
